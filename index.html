A little about me

Hi, thanks for taking the time to visit my page. I'm a new software developer who enjoys building all kinds of programs and exploring new technologies. Before starting a career as a developer, I was in the Canadian Armed Forces where I worked as a Combat Engineer. Being in the military was a very rewarding career. In 2017 I retired from the military after twelve years of service and moved back to Ontario with my wife and three children. Ever since retiring I have been pursuing a BSc in Computer Science at Western in London Ontario.
